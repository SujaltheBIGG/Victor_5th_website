Answers to our frequently asked questions

Airtwist helps you adopt AI with clarity — from strategy and prototyping to deployment, governance, and ongoing support.

How does Airtwist approach a new AI engagement?

We start with a discovery workshop to understand your goals, data, and constraints, then map the highest-impact AI use cases and a pragmatic roadmap to production.

Is your AI compliant with data privacy regulations?

Yes. We design every solution to meet GDPR and industry standards, with role-based access, data minimisation, encryption, and clear audit trails.

How do you integrate AI into our existing systems?

We connect to your existing data sources, tools, and cloud or on-prem infrastructure, then ship models through robust MLOps pipelines that fit your stack.

How do you keep AI models safe, fair, and unbiased?

We build in evaluation, bias testing, human-in-the-loop review, and explainability from day one, with continuous monitoring once a model is live.

Do you provide ongoing support after deployment?

Absolutely. Most engagements include monitoring, retraining, and optimisation so your AI keeps improving long after launch.
